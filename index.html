Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
To cancel your subscription, log in to your account and navigate to the "Subscription" section. From there, you can cancel your subscription.
How do I update my payment information?
To update your payment information, log in to your account and navigate to the "Payment" section. From there, you can update your payment information.
How long does shipping take?
Shipping times vary depending on your location and the shipping method you choose at checkout. Standard shipping usually takes 3-7 business days.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund.
How do I track my order?
To track your order, log in to your account and navigate to the "Orders" section. From there, you can view the status of your order and track your shipment.
